Who am I and what is my food truck about ?

Hello, my name is Pedro Alvàrez, a well-known chef specialized in Mexican cuisine and I am proud to present to you, “El Hombre Loco!”

“El Hombre Loco” is a food truck about Mexican food, with typicals meals such as tacos, quesadillas, enchilada and much more, customers can travel to Mexico throughout the meals i made with love, with fresh and high-quality products!

The name "El Hombre Loco", which means "The Crazy Man", comes from the fact that Mexican food is mostly spicy, so spicy that it will drive you insane!


My story


Since I was a child, my passion has always been food, especially cooking, I express myself through cooking.

When I was a teenager, I went to Mexico for vacations, I really liked the country, especially the food there, the tortillas, the quesadillas and the spices, when I came back from this trip, my passion for cooking had grown, and I decided to study food-related subjects to deepen it.

That’s also when the idea of making a mexican based food truck hit me. My motto is to share my passion of cooking and the mexican culture with the customers.
